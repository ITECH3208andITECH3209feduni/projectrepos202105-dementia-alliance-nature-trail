How To Play

Explore The Trail

Spot An Animal

Push The Camera Button

Scan The QR Code

Hear The Animal & Learn Their Story

Detailed Instructions

1. Explore the trail

2. Spot an animal statue and find its QR code

3. Press the camera button to activate the QR scanner

4. Point the camera at the QR code to instantly scan it

5. You have now unlocked the animal!

6. Hear its sound, listen to its story and learn exciting facts!

7. Find all the animals on the trial to finish the story!
