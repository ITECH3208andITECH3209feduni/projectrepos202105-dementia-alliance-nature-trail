Welcome Wander

Distance 450m

(All accessible)

Rest, Talk, Listen and Reflect

There are four small, all accessible linked side journeys to explore along the Welcome Wander.

Resting points along the way are spaced, providing a safe, inclusive, sensory experience. You are welcome to wander the trails and connect with nature.

It is suitable for wheel chairs and walking aids.
